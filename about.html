About the Artist

Treslynn Weedman is a contemporary visual artist whose work blends emotion, texture, and vibrant color. Each piece reflects a passion for self-expression and a deep love of visual storytelling.

Inspired by nature, memory, and personal experience, her paintings evoke a sense of warmth and mystery. She believes art should be felt as much as seen, and each canvas holds a story waiting to be discovered.

Based in Texas, Treslynn continues to expand her practice, creating pieces that invite viewers into her evolving journey.
